About Us

Planet Against Plastic is an organization with the goal of combatting plastic pollution,
more specifically single use plastics. Our main aim is to supply people with the resources and knowledge
they need to be able to aid us in this ongoing battle against plastic pollution.

Plastic pollution is one of the main threats against the environment and the wildlife around the world.
Throughout our page you will find shocking facts and images
that will show you some of the real time effects that these single use plastics are having on our planet.

How you can help

Here are a few very simple things you can do to help us save the planet;

Recycling is one of the easiest and most effective ways of fighting plastic pollution.
This can be done by simply keeping an extra containers for specific items such as glass and plastic.
Keeping these seperate and disposing of them in the correct bins massively helps the recycling process and the impact on land fills.

Community events are a more active and social way to combat plastic pollution.
There will be organzations in your local area that you can join, otherwise starting your own and posting on social media
to advertise and grow is a fantastic idea. The idea of these organzations is to create and lead events where people can meet and clean
public areas such as beaches, woods and parks.

Metal or wooden alternatives are also a great way to reduce waste.
Items that are easily substituted by these materials are straws, cutlery, water bottles.

If you struggle with or do not have the resources to follow the above advice the pictures in our gallery will take you too
some brilliant charities where a small donation can make a massive difference.
