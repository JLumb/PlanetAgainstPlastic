About Us

Planet Against Plastic is an organization with the goal of combatting plastic pollution
more specifically single use plastics. Our main aim is too supply people with the resources and knowledge
they need to be able to aid us in this ongoing battle against plastic pollution.

Plastic Pollution is one of the main threats against the environment and the wildlife around the world.
Throughout our page you will find shocking facts and images
that will show you some of the real time effects that these single use plastics are having on our planet.
